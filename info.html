you're a cat, stranded in the vast emptiness of space. Oxygen is running low, and your only chance of survival is to leap from bubble to bubble, fighting the clock to stay alive. Will you surrender to the void, or will you push yourself to the very end? It’s up to you to navigate this high-stakes adventure! Use your mouse to drag yourself between bubbles, but be quick—the oxygen won’t last forever. If you find it too challenging, hit the spacebar to zoom out and get a better view of your surroundings. Every bubble you reach gives you a precious breath of air, but watch out—if you miss your mark, you'll drift helplessly into the abyss of space. And don't forget about the angry aliens lurking around. They won’t be friendly if you collide with them, so steer clear or face the consequences! Survival’s not easy in space, but if you're smart, you might just find your way to the exit. Ready to take the leap?
